STUDY DESIGN

*A randomized, multicentre, double-blind, active-controlled study in adult patients with type 1 or type 2 diabetes presenting with central DME involvement in the study eye. Patients were assigned randomly in a 1:1:1 ratio to receive either EYLEA® 2 mg Q4 (n=155), EYLEA® 2 mg Q8 following 5 initial monthly doses (n=154) or active control laser (n=156). Beginning at Week 24, patients meeting a
pre-specified threshold of vision loss were eligible to receive additional treatment: patients in EYLEA® groups could receive laser and patients in laser group could receive EYLEA®. EYLEA® 2 mg Q4 is not a recommended dose.

†A randomized, multicentre, double-blind, active-controlled study in adult patients with type 1 or type 2 diabetes presenting with central DME involvement in the study eye. Patients were assigned randomly in a 1:1:1 ratio to receive either EYLEA® 2 mg Q4 (n=136), EYLEA® 2 mg Q8 following 5 initial monthly doses (n=135) or active control laser (n=135).
