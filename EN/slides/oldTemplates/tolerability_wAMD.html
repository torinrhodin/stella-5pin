DEMONSTRATED TOLERABILITY1

Serious adverse reactions related to the injection procedure in the Phase III AMD trials have occurred in less than 1 in 1000 intravitreal injections (22 of 36,206 injections) with either EYLEA® or ranibizumab, and included endophthalmitis, cataract traumatic, increased intraocular pressure, retinal detachment, and vitreous detachment.

Most common adverse reactions (ARs) reported in ≥5% of patients treated with EYLEA®*
* Combined safety data for VIEW 1 and VIEW 2 trials with up to 96 weeks’ exposure.
