Molecule Design†

Binding to VEGF-A and PGF1

VEGF-A and PGF are pro-angiogenic factors

  • VEGF acts via VEGFR 1 and VEGFR 2, present on the surface
    of endothelial cells
  • PGF binds only to VEGFR 1, present on the surface of endothelial
    cells and leukocytes
  • Excessive activation of VEGFR 1 and VEGFR 2 by VEGF-A can
    result in neovascularization and excessive vascular permeability
