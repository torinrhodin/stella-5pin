BRVO

TREAT-AND-EXTEND DOSING1

The recommended dose for EYLEA® is 2 mg (0.05 mL or 50 µL) administered by intravitreal injection:

  • Monthly (interval between doses should not be shorter than one month)*
  • Treatment interval may be extended up to 3 months based on visual and anatomic outcomes†
* Clinical trial experience of a monthly dosing regimen of 2 mg aflibercept beyond 6 months in the BRVO indication is limited.
† Prescribers should periodically assess (every 1 to 2 months) the need for continued therapy.
‡ For illustrative purposes only. Individual patient needs may vary.
