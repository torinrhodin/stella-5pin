+Study Design

STUDY DESIGN

*VIEW 1 was a randomized, multicentre, double-blind, active-controlled study in adult patients with wAMD. Patients were assigned in a 1:1:1:1 ratio to either EYLEA® 2 mg Q8 following 3 initial monthly doses (n=303), EYLEA® 2 mg Q4 (n=304), EYLEA® 0.5 mg Q4 (n=304) or ranibizumab 0.5 mg Q4 (n=306). This trial included doses that differ from the recommended dose of EYLEA®. The recommended dose of EYLEA® is 2 mg (0.05 mL, or 50 µL) administered by intravitreal injection monthly for 3 initial doses followed by one dose administered once every 2 months.

†VIEW 2 was a randomized, multicentre, double-blind, active-controlled study in adult patients with wAMD. Patients were assigned in a 1:1:1:1 ratio to either EYLEA® 2 mg Q8 following 3 initial monthly doses (n=313), EYLEA® 2 mg Q4 (n=313), EYLEA® 0.5 mg Q4 (n=311) or ranibizumab 0.5 mg Q4 (n=303). This trial included doses that differ from the recommended dose of EYLEA®. The recommended dose of EYLEA® is 2 mg (0.05 mL, or 50 µL) administered by intravitreal injection monthly for 3 initial doses followed by one dose administered once every 2 months.
