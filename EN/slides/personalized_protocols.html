Personalized protocols

An algorithm that allows technologists to personalize
injection protocols and provide consistent image quality
amongst clinicians

  • Allows technologists to account for unique patient
    and study variables.
  • Adjust contrast media
    protocols automatically
  • Personalize the volume and flow rate
    for each patient
  • Improve image quality
  • Configure according to the workflow,
    policies and preferences of each facility
PA=pulmonary angiography.
